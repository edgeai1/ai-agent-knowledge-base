## TL;DR

Marketed as the "first AI software engineer." Capable of planning, coding, debugging, and deploying end-to-end with its own shell, editor, and browser.

## Capabilities

- End-to-end software development
- Own shell, code editor, and browser
- Plans and executes multi-step development tasks
- Debugging and deployment

## Performance

- SWE-bench (full) at launch: 13.86% (SOTA at the time, March 2024)
- Devin 2.0: 45.8% (significant progress)
- Trails OpenHands and SWE-agent on benchmarks -- Cognition focuses on real-world usability

## Why It Matters

Sparked massive interest in coding agents. Defined the product category of "AI software engineer" and attracted $175M+ in funding.
