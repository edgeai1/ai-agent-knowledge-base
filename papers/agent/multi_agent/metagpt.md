## TL;DR

Encodes human SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) into multi-agent collaboration. Assigns roles like product manager, architect, engineer for collaborative software development.

## Method

- Multiple agents with defined roles (PM, architect, engineer, QA)
- Communication follows structured SOPs
- Agents produce standardized artifacts (PRD, design docs, code, tests)
- Reduces hallucination through structured workflows

## Why It Matters

Showed that structured role assignment and workflow design are critical for effective multi-agent collaboration. Influenced how production multi-agent systems are designed.
